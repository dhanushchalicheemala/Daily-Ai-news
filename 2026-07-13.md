[Unrot](https://unrot.co/blogs/today-top-10-ai-news-july-12-2026)

[Unrot](https://unrot.co/blogs/today-top-10-ai-news-july-12-2026)

## New Models & Tools

- **Google DeepMind sets Gemini 3.5 Pro launch for July 17** — Six weeks behind its original schedule, the model ships with a 2-million-token context window (2x the current frontier field), a "Deep Think" reasoning mode, and API pricing around $1.25 per million input tokens; it lands five days after GPT-5.6 and nine days after Grok 4.5. [Unrot](https://unrot.co/blogs/today-top-10-ai-news-july-12-2026)
- **Mistral open-sources Leanstral 1.5 for formal code/proof verification** — A 119B-parameter, Apache-2.0 Lean 4 code-agent model that hits 100% on the miniF2F formal-math benchmark and solves 587 of 672 PutnamBench problems; while scanning 57 open-source repos it surfaced five previously unknown bugs. [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/07/03/mistral-ai-releases-leanstral-1-5-an-apache-2-0-lean-4-code-agent-model-solving-587-of-672-putnambench-problems/) / [The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/mistrals-open-source-leanstral-1-5-aces-formal-math-benchmarks-and-catches-real-bugs-in-code/)
- **Google Search answer pages go fully AI-generated** — Traditional "ten blue links" results are being replaced by Gemini-generated answer pages with embedded citations. [Unrot](https://unrot.co/blogs/today-top-10-ai-news-july-12-2026)

## Company & Product Updates

- **Anthropic launches Claude Corps, a $150M nonprofit AI fellowship** — A fully funded, 12-month paid fellowship placing 1,000 early-career fellows (starting with a cohort of 100 in October) inside 400+ US nonprofits including RAINN, Goodwill Industries International, and Code for America; run in partnership with CodePath and Social Finance, with each host nonprofit also receiving a $10,000 grant and Claude credits. Applications for the first cohort close July 17.
