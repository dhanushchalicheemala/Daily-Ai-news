- **Ames Lab publishes AI-driven roadmap for permanent-magnet design** — Ames National Laboratory researchers detailed how combining physics-based modeling with AI reasoning can speed the search for rare-earth-free magnet materials by predicting magnetic properties directly from atomic and electronic structure.
