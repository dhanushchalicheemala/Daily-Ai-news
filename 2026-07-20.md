## Breakthroughs & Research

- **"Selective activation sparsity"** — a new training method that activates only the most relevant parameters per task — lets models match the performance of models roughly 3x their size. [Skycrumbs](https://skycrumbs.com/blog/ai-research-july-2026)
- **AI models are moving from predicting static protein structure to predicting protein dynamics** — how proteins change shape over time — giving a fuller picture for drug design; multiple research teams published results this month. [Skycrumbs](https://skycrumbs.com/blog/ai-research-july-2026)
- **Google DeepMind's mathematical reasoning system scored in the top 1% on IMO-style problems**, notable because these problems demand creative mathematical insight rather than pattern matching. [Skycrumbs](https://skycrumbs.com/blog/ai-research-july-2026)
- **Ames Laboratory's DuctGPT**, a physics-trained AI model, is being used to discover rare-earth-free permanent magnets — inventing new materials while factoring in production cost and component sourcing, rather than just interpolating from existing data. [Skycrumbs](https://skycrumbs.com/blog/ai-research-july-2026)
- **Anthropic's interpretability team used a Jacobian-based technique ("J-lens")** to identify a small internal subspace (~25 active concepts, under 10% of activation variance) in its models that behaves analogously to the "global workspace" concept from consciousness neuroscience. [ThursdAI](https://thursdai.news/releases/2026-07)
