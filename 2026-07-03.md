## IPOs & Corporate Moves

- **Anthropic's self-reported revenue overtakes OpenAI's** — Anthropic said it is on track for roughly $47B in annualized revenue and expects to be profitable in 2029, a year ahead of OpenAI's own profitability target; OpenAI is pacing toward $25–33B in annualized revenue. The shift is being driven largely by enterprise adoption of Claude Code. ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/07/02/sam-altman-new-world-order-ai-openai-google-anthropic/))

- **More high-profile researchers leave Google DeepMind for Anthropic** — Google DeepMind director John Jumper, along with researchers Jonas Adler and Alexander Pritzel — all key contributors to Google's Gemini work — are departing for Anthropic, continuing a talent-migration trend from Google to its rivals. ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-24/google-poised-to-lose-two-more-high-profile-ai-staffers-to-anthropic), [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/24/ai-researchers-continue-to-leave-google-for-its-rivals/))
