## Breakthroughs & Research

- **Reasoning-model research points to self-correction training, not scale, as key driver** — Researchers at MIT and Stanford published findings that success on hard math/logic problems for reasoning models correlates more with how models are trained to self-correct mid-reasoning than with raw parameter count.
- **AI protein dynamics modeling advances** — Separate research groups at the University of Cambridge and UCSF published complementary approaches to predicting not just static protein structure but how proteins change shape over time and how that relates to function.
