## Company & Product Updates

- **GitHub Copilot's first full usage-based billing cycle closes** — June 30 marked the end of Copilot's first complete 30-day cycle under its new "AI Credits" model, which replaced flat-rate Premium Request Units on June 1. Base subscription prices held steady (Pro at $10/month, Pro+ at $39/month), but included credit allotments shifted, with some heavy agentic users reporting sharply higher effective costs. ([Build Fast With AI](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-june-30-2026))

- **Alphabet's $84.75B AI infrastructure raise** — Alphabet's equity capital raise, upsized from an initial $80B to $84.75B at pricing, stands as the largest equity financing in corporate history by a major technology company, earmarked for AI compute buildout. ([Build Fast With AI](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-june-30-2026))
