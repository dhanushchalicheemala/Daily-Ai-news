## IPOs & Corporate Moves

### SpaceX Completes Largest IPO in History
SpaceX debuted on the Nasdaq on June 12, 2026 under the ticker **SPCX**, priced at $135/share. The stock surged 19% on its first trading day, closing at $160.95 and pushing its market cap above $2 trillion — shattering Saudi Aramco's prior record of $35.4 billion raised. The offering raised $75 billion at a $1.75 trillion valuation. MSCI announced SPCX would begin entering its large-cap indices starting June 13, the first full trading day post-IPO. COO Gwynne Shotwell rang the Nasdaq closing bell to mark the occasion after 24 years as a private company.
