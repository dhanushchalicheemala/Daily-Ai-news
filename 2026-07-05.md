## Breakthroughs & Research

- **Photonic AI platform matches expert-level medical diagnostics** — A team led by Professor Han Zhang at Shenzhen University built an all-fiber photonic computing platform using black-phosphorus tunable modulators, achieving expert-level accuracy on retinal detachment and liver cancer diagnosis while running roughly 246x more energy-efficient than conventional GPUs. ([ScienceDaily-linked coverage via DeepUseCase](https://deepusecase.com/blog/latest-ai-research-breakthroughs-2026/))

- **Study finds attention-test flaw in top models** — Researchers applied a classic psychology-based attention test to leading LLMs and found performance held up on short lists but deteriorated sharply as list length and task complexity grew, pointing to a durable weakness in long-context attention handling. ([llm-stats.com](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news))

- **UN Global Dialogue on AI Governance opens in Geneva July 6** — Member states convene this week to discuss international coordination on AI safety and governance; Jensen Huang, Andy Jassy, and Brad Smith have joined the UN's first-ever multilateral AI governance commission, co-chaired by Marc Benioff and Rwandan President Paul Kagame. ([UN News](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/07/1167848))
