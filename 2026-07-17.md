## New Models & Tools

- **Moonshot AI ships Kimi K3.** Moonshot AI released Kimi K3 on July 16, a 2.8-trillion-parameter open mixture-of-experts model built on Kimi Delta Attention and Attention Residuals, activating 16 of 896 experts per token. [llm-stats.com](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news)
- **Thinking Machines Lab debuts Inkling.** Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab released its first in-house model, Inkling, a 975B-parameter mixture-of-experts system that draws on roughly 41B active parameters per task, doubling down on a bet against one-size-fits-all AI. [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/15/thinking-machines-amps-up-its-bet-against-one-size-fits-all-ai-with-its-first-open-model-inkling/)
- **OpenAI's GPT-5.6 family rolls out.** OpenAI's new flagship family ships in three sizes — Luna, Terra, and Sol — priced from $1–$5 per million input tokens, all with a 1M-token context window; GPT-5.6 is also being added to Microsoft 365 Copilot. [llm-stats.com](https://llm-stats.com/llm-updates)
- **Google releases Nano Banana 2 Lite and Gemini Omni Flash.** Google shipped two new lightweight model variants aimed at faster, cheaper multimodal workloads. [Crescendo](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
- **xAI Voice Agent Builder launches.** xAI's no-code Voice Agent Builder can produce production-ready voice agents in under two minutes, priced at $0.05/minute of audio plus $0.01/minute for telephony. [Crescendo](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
- **AWS makes AgentCore Harness generally available.** The managed service lets developers deploy Bedrock-based AI agents by configuration with a single command, cutting the boilerplate needed to stand up agents. [Crescendo](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
