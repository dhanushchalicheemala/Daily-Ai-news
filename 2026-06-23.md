## Breakthroughs & Research

- **AI resolves 80-year-old math conjecture** — The Automated Conjecture Resolution (ACR) framework paired a reasoning agent searching for proofs with a second agent formalizing them in Lean 4, overturning an open problem in commutative algebra with "essentially no human involvement." The system also discovered new counterexamples in algebraic groups and p-adic Hodge theory. ([Source](https://www.buildthisnow.com/blog/guide/mechanics/ai-research-june-2026))

- **AI-accelerated climate models (25× faster)** — Researchers demonstrated climate models that run approximately 25 times faster by combining generative AI methods with physics-based data, significantly reducing the compute time needed for climate simulations. ([Source](https://blog.mean.ceo/latest-ai-breakthroughs-news-june-2026/))

- **MycoBCP deep learning for tuberculosis** — A deep learning system called MycoBCP is helping researchers detect subtle tuberculosis cell changes to speed up treatment discovery and drug resistance identification. ([Source](https://blog.mean.ceo/latest-ai-breakthroughs-news-june-2026/))

- **NIST Safe Step fire evacuation AI** — NIST researchers developed Safe Step (released June 4), an AI model that identifies safe evacuation routes during fires, designed to work with dynamic emergency exit display systems in real time. ([Source](https://www.devflokers.com/blog/ai-tech-news-model-releases-june-2026))
