# AI News — 2026-07-23

## Startups & Funding

- **CuspAI raised a $450M Series B** led by Kleiner Perkins and NEA, with significant participation from Jeff Bezos's Bezos Expeditions, valuing the two-year-old Cambridge (UK) materials-discovery startup at $2.6B — up from $520M last September. The company also launched the **AI Materials Foundry**, a coalition of 48+ tech, industrial, and research partners (including Meta and Hyundai) applying AI to semiconductor materials, carbon capture, water treatment, and energy systems. [Tech Funding News](https://techfundingnews.com/cuspai-400m-series-b-2-6b-valuation-bezos-materials-search/), [Electronics Weekly](https://www.electronicsweekly.com/news/business/cambridge-startup-using-ai-for-materials-research-raises-450m-2026-07/)
- **North American startup funding hit record highs in H1 2026**, with AI-related investment totaling roughly $392B across the US and Canada — nearly 88% of it flowing to US-based companies, concentrated in foundation models, AI infrastructure, defense/autonomy, and vertical AI applications. [Crunchbase News](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/na-startup-funding-ma-shattered-records-ai-q2-2026/)

## IPOs & Corporate Moves

- **AMD's "Advancing AI 2026" conference concluded in San Francisco (July 22–23)**, opening with the debut of **Zen 6-based EPYC "Venice" CPUs** — the first x86 server chip built on TSMC's 2nm process — followed by the commercial launch of the **Instinct MI450-series accelerators** (MI455X, MI450X, and MI430X) and the double-wide **Helios rack-scale AI system** (72 GPUs, 31TB HBM4 memory, ~$5.5M per rack, shipping Q3 2026). Oracle is set to be the first cloud provider offering a public AI supercluster built on 50,000 MI450 GPUs. [Tech Times](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/321257/20260722/amd-advancing-ai-2026-opens-zen-6-venice-helios-open-ai-rack-bet.htm), [Wccftech](https://wccftech.com/amd-helios-ai-rack-mi455x-6th-gen-epyc-challenging-nvidia/)
- **Microsoft and Mistral expanded their strategic partnership** with a new multibillion-dollar commitment (amount undisclosed) to build out Mistral's European AI infrastructure using thousands of Nvidia Vera Rubin GPUs. Microsoft is funding the buildout without taking a new equity stake, and Mistral's Medium 3.5 and OCR 4 models are now available in Microsoft Foundry. [Microsoft Source](https://news.microsoft.com/source/2026/07/21/microsoft-and-mistral-expand-strategic-partnership-to-give-enterprises-and-regulated-industries-frontier-ai-they-can-control/)

## New Models & Tools

- **Moonshot AI's Kimi K3**, a 2.8-trillion-parameter open-weight mixture-of-experts model (16 of 896 experts active per token), continues to draw attention ahead of its full open-weight release on July 27. It introduces Kimi Delta Attention and Attention Residuals for faster long-context decoding (up to 6.3x at 1M tokens), supports native vision, and is reported to beat Claude Fable 5 on the Frontend Code Arena benchmark — priced at $3/M input and $15/M output tokens. [Tom's Hardware](https://www.tomshardware.com/tech-industry/artificial-intelligence/moonshot-releases-2-8-trillion-parameter-kimi-k3), [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/07/16/moonshot-ai-releases-kimi-k3-a-2-8-trillion-parameter-open-moe-model-with-kimi-delta-attention-and-1m-context/)

## Breakthroughs & Research

- **"Selective activation sparsity"** remains a key theme out of ICML 2026 discussion — a training technique letting models match the performance of models ~3x their size by activating only task-relevant parameters, reinforcing the industry's efficiency-over-scale push amid the ongoing price war. [Skycrumbs](https://skycrumbs.com/blog/ai-research-july-2026)

## Company & Product Updates

- **OpenAI introduced "Presence,"** an enterprise platform for deploying trusted, guardrailed AI agents across voice and chat, announced July 22. Deployments are hand-rolled by OpenAI's Forward Deployed Engineers or select systems integrators (not self-service), starting with narrow jobs like billing, insurance claims, or IT service requests. OpenAI says the system now handles roughly 75% of its own inbound support requests, matching human help-desk quality within weeks of rollout. [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-presence/), [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/openai-unveils-presence-a-new-platform-that-lets-enterprises-launch-and-manage-realtime-voice-agents-and-chatbots)
- **OpenAI also launched "ChatGPT for Small Businesses"** on July 21, pairing ChatGPT Work (a multi-step agentic tool powered by GPT-5.6) with resources aimed at helping small businesses adopt AI for productivity and scaling.
- **A new HCLTech survey of 500 enterprise decision-makers** found 90% report GenAI/agentic AI is transforming workflows, but only 18% say it's delivering significant revenue impact yet — underscoring a continued gap between AI adoption and measurable ROI.
