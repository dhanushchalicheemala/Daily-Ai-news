[Crescendo AI](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)

- **Google Co-Scientist (Nature)** — Google published a paper in *Nature* describing a multi-agent system built on Gemini for structured scientific hypothesis generation and knowledge discovery, accelerating the pace of scientific research.
