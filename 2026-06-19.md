## Breakthroughs & Research

- **Google TurboQuant Reduces LLM Memory Overhead**: Google's research team unveiled TurboQuant at ICLR 2026, an algorithm that significantly reduces the memory overhead caused by the KV cache — one of the biggest bottlenecks in running large AI models at scale. ([ScienceDaily](https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/computers_math/artificial_intelligence/))

- **Climate Models Run 25× Faster with Generative AI**: UC San Diego researchers demonstrated climate models running approximately 25 times faster by combining generative AI methods with physics-based data, unlocking new possibilities for climate science. - **MycoBCP Deep Learning Accelerates TB Treatment**: A deep learning system called MycoBCP is helping researchers detect subtle tuberculosis cell changes, speeding up treatment discovery in a disease that still kills over a million people a year.
