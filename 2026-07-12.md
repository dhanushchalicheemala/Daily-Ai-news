## New Models & Tools

- **OpenAI launches ChatGPT Work** — A GPT-5.6-powered agent that takes a stated goal, gathers context across connected apps/files, breaks it into steps, and independently produces finished sheets, slides, docs, and web apps over hours-long sessions; positioned as OpenAI's direct answer to Anthropic's Claude Cowork. [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/openai-introduces-chatgpt-work-a-cloud-based-ai-agent-that-manages-tasks-across-email-slack-and-calendars)
- **Mistral open-sources Leanstral 1.5, a Lean 4 formal-verification model** — Apache 2.0-licensed, 119B-parameter MoE model (6B active) hits 100% on miniF2F and solves 587/672 Putnam problems; found five previously unknown bugs scanning 57 open-source repos, operating as a code agent that edits files and queries the Lean language server directly. [the-decoder](https://the-decoder.com/mistrals-open-source-leanstral-1-5-aces-formal-math-benchmarks-and-catches-real-bugs-in-code/)
- **GLM-5.2 (Z.ai) posts 80x customer growth and 27x daily token volume on Vercel in its first full week** — The open-weight model scores 62.1% on SWE-bench Pro (ahead of GPT-5.5's 58.6%) at roughly one-sixth the cost of comparable flagship models, drawing praise from Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch. [officechai](https://officechai.com/ai/this-changes-things-open-source-glm-5-2-impresses-vercel-box-ceos/)
