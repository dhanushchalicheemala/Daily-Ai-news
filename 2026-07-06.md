[Ames Laboratory](https://www.ameslab.gov/news/ames-lab-scientist-provides-ai-driven-roadmap-for-future-permanent-magnet-design)
- **Z.ai's GLM-5.2 fuels "China catching up" debate** — The inexpensive Chinese model is drawing attention for performance competitive with leading Anthropic and OpenAI models, intensifying discussion of narrowing the US-China AI gap. [LLM Stats](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news)
- **Five-country agencies issue joint guidance on agentic AI risk** — Cybersecurity and intelligence agencies from the US, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, and the UK released "Careful Adoption of Agentic AI Services," flagging five risk categories — privilege, design/configuration, behavior, structural, and accountability — for agentic AI in critical infrastructure and defense. [Crescendo AI](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
