# AI News Digest — July 9, 2026

## Startups & Funding

- **Kaon AI raises $60M** to build a "personalized story engine" — a generative AI platform for personalized story worlds — in a round backed by B Capital, Redpoint Ace, Goodwater Capital, and DCM. [Source](https://variety.com/2026/digital/news/ai-startup-kaon-ai-raises-60-million-funding-round-1236803914/)
- **Reflection AI activates a $6.3B compute lease** at SpaceX's Colossus 2 facility in Memphis, locking in Nvidia GB300 chips through 2029 to train American open-weight frontier models. [Source](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
- Global startup funding hit a record **$510B in H1 2026**, with AI driving the surge; OpenAI and Anthropic alone accounted for $217B — 43% of all H1 startup funding — and roughly 88% of AI funding continues flowing to US-based companies. [Source](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/global-startup-exits-ipo-ma-soar-ai-q2-h1-2026/)

## IPOs & Corporate Moves

- **Anthropic overtakes OpenAI in business subscriptions** and self-reported revenue trajectory, with run-rate revenue surpassing $30B (up from ~$9B at the end of 2025) and a path to $47B and profitability by 2029 — a year ahead of OpenAI's own projections. [Source](https://fortune.com/2026/07/02/sam-altman-new-world-order-ai-openai-google-anthropic/)
- **Google cuts its AI Plus subscription price** from $7.99 to $4.99/month as the OpenAI–Anthropic–Google price war intensifies, with OpenAI also weighing steep token-pricing cuts to defend enterprise share. [Source](https://sherwood.news/tech/openai-anthropic-google-price-wars-where-no-one-is-making-money/)
- **Talent war escalates**: Anthropic researchers Jonas Adler and Alexander Pritzel, both key Gemini contributors, are leaving Google for Anthropic, while veteran researcher Noam Shazeer is departing Google for OpenAI. [Source](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/24/ai-researchers-continue-to-leave-google-for-its-rivals/)
- **Sam Altman calls for a "new world order" for AI** as OpenAI cedes ground to Google and Anthropic on revenue and enterprise adoption. [Source](https://fortune.com/2026/07/02/sam-altman-new-world-order-ai-openai-google-anthropic/)

## New Models & Tools

- **OpenAI ships gpt-realtime-2.1 and gpt-realtime-2.1-mini**, cutting p95 latency on Realtime voice models by at least 25% and improving recognition, noise handling, reasoning, and tool use; GPT-4.5 has been fully retired from ChatGPT. [Source](https://releasebot.io/updates/openai)
- **xAI launches a Voice Agent Builder** that creates production-ready voice agents in under two minutes with no code, priced at $0.05/minute of audio plus $0.01/minute for telephony. [Source](https://x.ai/news)
- **Alteryx debuts Agent Studio and an MCP Server** at its Inspire 2026 conference, letting business analysts turn existing data workflows into autonomous agents connected to tools like Slack and Microsoft Teams. [Source](https://agentic.ai/news)
- **HPE expands its AI Factory portfolio with NVIDIA**, adding the NVIDIA Vera CPU for agent orchestration and NVIDIA Agent Toolkit for managing autonomous multi-agent systems in production. [Source](https://www.aiapps.com/blog/top-ai-news-july-breakthroughs-launches-trends/)

## Breakthroughs & Research

- **AI-designed vaccine component completes initial human trials** in a University of Cambridge study — an early but notable proof point for AI-driven biomedical and drug design. [Source](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
- **AI simulation speeds up neutron-star-merger modeling**, giving researchers a much faster way to model how these mergers forge many of the universe's heaviest elements and improving predictions of the resulting explosions. [Source](https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/computers_math/artificial_intelligence/)
- **JetSpec posts a 9.64x speedup on MATH-500 benchmarks** using a token look-ahead prediction technique, pointing to further inference-efficiency gains for reasoning models. [Source](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)
- **Anthropic proposes an industry-wide jailbreak-severity scoring framework**, working with Amazon, Microsoft, Google, and other partners to standardize how model providers rate and report jailbreak risk. [Source](https://www.anthropic.com/news)
