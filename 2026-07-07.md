## Breakthroughs & Research

- **AI-designed vaccine completes human trials.** University of Cambridge researchers report the first vaccine whose key component was designed entirely by AI, successfully completing initial human trials. [Source](https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/computers_math/artificial_intelligence/)
- **Photonic AI chip for medical diagnostics.** Shenzhen University researchers built an all-fiber photonic AI platform using black-phosphorus tunable modulators, hitting expert-level accuracy on retinal detachment and liver cancer diagnosis while running 246x more efficiently than conventional GPUs. [Source](https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/computers_math/artificial_intelligence/)
- **Climate modeling breakthrough.** UC San Diego and the Allen Institute for AI's "Spherical DYffusion" model projects 100 years of climate patterns in 25 hours — 25x faster than existing physics-based methods. [Source](https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/computers_math/artificial_intelligence/)
